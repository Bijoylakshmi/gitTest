Dermot Casey Tree Surgeons & Tree Removals Services

Dermot Casey is a family run Tree Surgeon business, providing a full range of Arborist services for over 40 years. We have a loyal & satisfied customer base from large utility contracts to private garden jobs carried out Nationwide. We also provide a nationwide tree removal service. All work listed is available for commercial and residential needs. Nationally we are well renowned for our reliability, professionalism and quality. As a family run business, our customer care is personal, making sure your needs come first. Our Arborist services include tree surgery, tree removal, pruning, trimming, mulching, chipping, crown reduction & stump grinding. We also carry out surveys, reports, site clearance, bridge, motorway & railway maintenance & offer a 24-hour emergency call out service throughout Ireland. We only use modern specialised equipment to get the job completed to perfection.
